Logistic Regression & Log-Linear Models

pdf for Logistic Regression & Log-Linear Models

  • Logistic Regression

    • For customers who came into a car dealership, We explored the relationship between if they bought a car and the age of the current car using logistic regression. We concluded that there is an influence on age of currently owned car on the odds of buying a car.

  • Log-Linear Models

    • Using log-linear models, we examined the dependence of the variables: age (young, old), gender(male, female), IQ(high, low). We used model selection with the AIC criteria to fit a model to our data, then tested whether we can drop the terms. We determined if each interaction term has an effect by using the Wald confidence intervals for the interaction terms. We concluded that age and gender were independent but age and IQ are dependent.
