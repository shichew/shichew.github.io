PDF for Webscraping Project

StackEchange was webscraped for information(type of entry, user's name, user id, date, reputation, votes, HTML content, and unique identifier) on each post to answer questions such as the number of people who answer posts, common tags, posts with specific words such as HTML, and finding the number of functions referenced in the titles.
